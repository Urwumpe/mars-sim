MarsNet

MarsNet is a chat box located at the left end of the Status Bar.

It is a new, experimental feature that allows users to interact with the 'A.I. agent' that inhabits the MarsNet, a smart globalnet on future Mars.

You may click the MarNet button to open a chat box.

In the textfield, enter the name of a person or a bot and choose one from the drop-down box. Next, click Enter or the 'Broadcast' button to initiate your query.

Once the connection has been established, enter a number from 0 to 15 to ask the following questions :

0 : exit the conversation
1 : what is your Location State ? are you inside a vehicle or in a particular building ?
2 : what are you doing ? what task are you performing ?
3 : where do you stay ? where is your designated quarters/bed, in what building, and at which settlement ?
4 : what is your Container unit ? are you inside a vehicle or a settlement ?
5 : what is your Top Container unit ? are you inside a vehicle or a settlement ?
6 : what is your Location Situation ? are you inside a building, outside the settlement and/or in a vehicle ?
7 : what vehicle are you in and how is it ?
8 : what building are you at ?
9 : which settlement are you at ?
10 : what is your associated settlement ? which settlement is your hometown on Mars ?
11 : which settlement was he/she buried?
12 : where is the location of the vehicle that you are in ?
13 : which settlement does the vehicle that you are at belong to ?
14 : is the vehicle you are in still inside a garage? Which garage ?
15 : does the vehicle you are in currently park at a settlement ? Which settlement ?

If you enter the name of a settlement in the textfield, enter a number from 0 to 15 to ask the following questions :

0 : Exit the conversation
1 : how many beds are there in total ?
2 : how many open beds that have already been designated to a person ?
3 : how many empty beds that are currently NOT occupied ?
4 : how many people are currently sleeping in his/her quarters ?

Note : you may type '0' or 'quit' or 'bye' or 'exit' and hit enter to leave the conversation.
