Food Recipe - Make Cranberry Sauce from Cranberry

Description

Add sugar, salt, ginger to cranberries and cook in the pan over low heat, stirring occasionally for 10 mins, until the sugar dissolves and the cranberries are soft. Increase the heat to medium and cook until the cranberries burst in a few minutes. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the reserved cranberries.
