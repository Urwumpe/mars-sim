Food - Make Quinoa Sprouts from Quinoa

Description

Rinse the quinoa seeds in a bowl. Wash the quinoa seeds to remove the soapy coating known as saponin. Soak in water and drain the water after 30 minutes. Repeat the rinsing and draining process every 8 hours. Put the tray of quinoa in a dark room. Place the cloth back over the quinoa for protection.The quinoa will sprout into a plate full of spiral-shaped roots that are at least one-quarter inch in length. Dry out sprouts for up to 12 hours after fully germinated.
