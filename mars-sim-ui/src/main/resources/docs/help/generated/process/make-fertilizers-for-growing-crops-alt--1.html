Process - Make fertilizers for growing crops Alt #1

Description

A. 3 main macronutrients (NPK): Nitrogen (N): leaf growth; Phosphorus (P): development of roots, flowers, seeds, fruit; Potassium (K): strong stem growth, movement of water in plants, promotion of flowering and fruiting; B. 3 secondary macronutrients: calcium (Ca), magnesium (Mg), and sulfur (S); C. Micronutrients: copper (Cu), iron (Fe), manganese (Mn), molybdenum (Mo), zinc (Zn), boron (B). D. Of occasional significance are silicon (Si), cobalt (Co), and vanadium (V).
