Resource - Silica

Description

The word Silica came from the Latin silex. Also known as silicon dioxide, silica is an oxide of silicon with the chemical formula SiO2, most commonly found in nature as quartz and in various living organisms. In many parts of the world, silica is the major constituent of sand.
