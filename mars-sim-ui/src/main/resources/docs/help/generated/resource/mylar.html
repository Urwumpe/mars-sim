Resource - Mylar

Description

Mylar is paper that is coated with or made from a polyester film BoPET material. BoPET (biaxially-oriented polyethylene terephthalate) is a polyester film made from stretched polyethylene terephthalate (PET). Mylar has a high tensile strength and is very durable yet flexible, making it perfect for packing materials, electrical insulation, solar technology and countless other practical uses.
