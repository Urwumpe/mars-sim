Resource - Rhizobia

Description

Seen as organic fertilizer, the nitrogen fixing bacteria that colonize the roots of their preferred plant partner and take nitrogen from the air and convert it into ammonia, a form of nitrogen that plants can use. In return, legumes feed the bacteria a steady diet of plant sugars.
