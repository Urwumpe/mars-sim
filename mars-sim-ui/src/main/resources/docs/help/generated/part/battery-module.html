Part - Battery Module

Description

Assuming the future achievement of 1 kg/kWh or 1 kWh/kg, this 15kWh rechargeable battery provides 'auxiliary' electrical power to the vehicle, on top of the primary fuel cell stack. It operates at 400V. With 15 kWh capacity, or 37.5 Ah (= 15/.4). Assume 6.43 km/kWh (based on a Tesla Model S battery pack weighing 2240 kg with 100 kWh capacity that can travel 643 km), this battery allows traveling a distance of 96.5 km on a single charge.
