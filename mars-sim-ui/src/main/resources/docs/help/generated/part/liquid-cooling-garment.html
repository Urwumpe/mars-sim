Part - Liquid Cooling Garment

Description

The innermost layer of the EVA suit. Provides thermal control by circulating air and water cooled by a sublimator over crew member's body. Can handle peak loads of up to 500 kcal/hr for 15 minutes, 400 kcal/hr for up to an hour, or 250 kcal/hr for up to 7 hours.
