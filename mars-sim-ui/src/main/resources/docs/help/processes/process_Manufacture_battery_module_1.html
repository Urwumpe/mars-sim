Process : "Manufacture battery module 1"

1. Description :

  • At operating voltage of 4.2V, the lithium content is 0.075g/Wh. For a cathode material (LiMn1.5Ni0.5O4) on a lithium ion battery, when cycled at C/30 in the 2.5-4.3V voltage range, the theoretical capacity of LiNMC Q_specific is 165 mAh g-1. or 160 Ah kg-1. At 3.8V, 3.8V * 160 mAh g-1 = 608 Wh kg-1. Content is 1.6447 kg/kWh.
